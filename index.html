Section 1

What Is Profound?

Profound (tryprofound.com) has emerged as a specialized, full-stack AEO/GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) platform. Launched in 2024, it is designed to help brands monitor, understand, and influence their visibility within AI-generated answers across more than ten of the most popular engines (including GPT-5.2, Perplexity, Claude, and Google's AI Overviews).

As of February 2026, the company has demonstrated significant market traction, securing $58.5M in funding (including a $35M Series B), achieving an estimated $30M+ in Annual Recurring Revenue, and being named a Leader in G2's Winter 2026 AEO category.

◆
Proprietary Data Moat
A real-user conversation panel of over 400 million anonymized monthly prompts, providing unique, directional data on what real people are asking AI models. This powers its "Prompt Volumes" feature — akin to search volume for the AI era.
◇
Comprehensive Monitoring
Through active front-end probing and server-side analytics, Profound tracks brand citations, sentiment, share-of-voice, and the technical behavior of AI crawlers on a brand's website.
◊
Workflow Automation ("Agents")
Originally called "Workflows" and rebranded in January 2026, these are node-based visual builders that allow for the automation of specific marketing tasks triggered by AEO insights.
As a relatively early-stage product, some user reviews mention that the Profound UI can occasionally be buggy or slow, which is not uncommon for a platform that is rapidly adding new features.

Section 1 (continued)

Why Profound Does Not Replace Manus

While Profound's "Agents" use the same terminology as a general-purpose tool like Manus, their function and scope are fundamentally different. Manus is a domain-agnostic, autonomous AI agent operating within a sandboxed cloud environment. It is Turing-complete, meaning it can solve any computable problem, and possesses the tools to execute open-ended tasks, including full web browsing, code execution, file system manipulation, and interaction with external APIs.

Profound's Agents are best understood as vertical workflow automations, whereas Manus is a horizontal, general-purpose execution engine.

The most effective operational model involves leveraging both systems in a synergistic "Profound on top of Manus" stack. The two systems are not in competition; they are two essential halves of a complete, modern marketing operation.

Section 2

Profound vs. Competitors

The AEO space is competitive, with 10-15 viable tools available by 2026. However, a clear market hierarchy has emerged. Profound leads in overall data depth and market traction, but several competitors offer strong, specialized alternatives that may be a better fit depending on an organization's specific needs, budget, and priorities.

The following comparison evaluates Profound against its top three competitors: AirOps, Scrunch, and Peec AI.

While Profound's lead is significant today, a competitor could theoretically build a similar user panel, or a new data-gathering paradigm could emerge, reducing Profound's long-term defensibility.

Recommended Stack Manus-only (+ optional free tools like HubSpot AEO Grader, or Otterly Lite for $29/mo). Manus + Peec AI (€89-€199/mo) or AirOps ($0-199/mo). Manus + Profound Growth ($399/mo).
What This Tier Provides Manus can perform weekly, automated audits of the top 5-10 queries across major AI platforms. A scheduled task queries the platforms, parses responses for mentions of Alpha School and competitors, and compiles findings into a spreadsheet and summary report. This requires approximately 15-30 minutes per week of team oversight and introduces no new tooling costs. A dedicated monitoring tool (Peec AI or AirOps) provides structured, daily data on visibility, sentiment, and citations across multiple engines. This data can be fed into Manus via API for automated follow-up actions such as competitive research and content drafting. Initial setup requires 2-4 hours per week, dropping to approximately 1 hour once workflows are established. Profound's proprietary data moat (400M+ real user conversations, Prompt Volumes, crawler analytics) is integrated with Manus as the execution engine via MCP server or Python SDK. Profound's Agents handle continuous monitoring and opportunity identification; Manus handles the complex, multi-step execution (research, content creation, publishing, campaign management). This requires approximately 1-2 hours per week of strategic oversight.
Expected Outcomes A clear, objective baseline of Alpha School's current AI visibility at zero incremental cost. Key data points include: weekly share-of-voice percentage, positive/negative/neutral mention counts, and identification of top competitors per query. This baseline is valuable regardless of whether a formal AEO strategy is pursued. Structured, daily monitoring data that enables trend analysis over time. Benchmarks from industry peers suggest 20-45% visibility improvements are achievable with consistent optimization. The data from this tier provides the evidence needed to make a well-informed decision about whether to scale further. Access to the deepest available AEO intelligence in the market, combined with autonomous execution. Organizations at this tier have reported 50-80%+ share-of-voice on core terms and significant reductions in customer acquisition costs. This tier represents the maximum capability currently available in the AEO tooling landscape.

The decision of how much to invest in AEO is a strategic one that depends on Alpha School's broader marketing priorities, budget, and competitive landscape. At every tier, Manus remains the execution backbone; the question is simply how much specialized AEO intelligence to layer on top of it. If no AEO strategy has yet been set, starting at the Low Priority tier to gather baseline data before committing to a larger investment is a sound, low-risk approach.
